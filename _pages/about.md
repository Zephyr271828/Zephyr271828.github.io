Hi there, I'm Yufeng (Felix) Xu (许御风). I'm a junior@NYU Shanghai majoring in Computer Science.

In 2022, I graduated from Suzhou High School of Jiangsu Province(江苏省苏州中学), and entered NYU Shanghai with full scholarship. In 2023 June, I joined Prof. Keith W Ross and Prof. Li Guo's group to study the Neural Collapse phenomenon in Deep Learning. Since 2024 February, I've been working with Prof. Tianyi Zhou and Prof. Shengjie Wang's on multimodal diffusion for world model. In the mean time, I joined Prof. Chen Zhao's research group to work on constrained decoding for Tool LLMs.

My research interest lies in mutlimodal generative models. If you want to reach out to me, feel free to contact me with my email yx3038[at]nyu[dot]edu.
