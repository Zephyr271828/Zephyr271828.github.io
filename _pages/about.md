Hi there, I'm Yufeng (Felix) Xu (许御风). I'm a rising junior@NYU Shanghai majoring in Computer Science.

In 2022, I graduated from Suzhou High School of Jiangsu Province(江苏省苏州中学), and entered NYU Shanghai with full scholarship. In 2023 June, I joined Prof. Keith W Ross and Prof. Li Guo's research group and did a research on Neural Collapse in Deep Learning. Since 2024 February, I started to work in Prof. Tianyi Zhou and Prof. Shengjie Wang's lab on bidirectional diffusion model. In the mean time, I joined Prof. Chen Zhao's research group to work on a comparision study between Tool LLMs and constrained decoding of LLMs. 

My research interest lies in mutlimodal generative models, including but not limited to diffusion models and LLMs. If you want to reach out to me, feel free to contact me with my email yx3038[at]nyu[dot]edu.
